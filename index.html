Pokemon Go power-leveling

The most efficient way to gain experience points in Pokemon Go is to save all your evolutions and do them while you use a Lucky Egg. Unfortunately, a lucky egg only lasts for 30 minutes. Use this calculator to make the most of your time!

  1. Acquire a lucky egg. You will get one when you reach level 8, 9, 10, 15. You will get two on level 20. (reference)

  2. Don't evolve any Pokemon. Save up all your evolvables!

  3. Once you get enough evolutions (around 150 Pidgey candies), use this calculator to get step-by-step instructions on how to make the most of your Lucky Egg.
